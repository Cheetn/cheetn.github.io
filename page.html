Learning with SkillsFuture

SkillsFuture is a Singaporean initiative that provides opportunities for lifelong learning and skill enhancement. It offers various courses in technology, digital literacy, and more.

Benefits of SkillsFuture

  • Subsidized learning for Singaporeans
  • Wide range of courses available
  • Enhance digital literacy skills
  • Flexible learning options
  • Applicable for all age groups
