Video Calling and Messaging Guide

How to Use WhatsApp for Messaging and Video Calling

Step 1: Download and Install WhatsApp

Go to the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android) and search for "WhatsApp." Download and install the app.

Step 2: Set Up Your Account

  • Open WhatsApp and agree to the terms.
  • Enter your phone number and verify it with the SMS code sent to you.
  • Set up your profile by adding your name and a profile picture.

Step 3: Sending a Message

  • Tap on the chat icon.
  • Select a contact or tap on the new chat button to start a conversation.
  • Type your message in the text box and tap the send button.

Step 4: Making a Video Call

  • Open a chat with the person you want to call.
  • Tap the video camera icon at the top of the screen.
  • Wait for the other person to answer the call.
  • Tap the red phone button to end the call.

How to Use WeChat for Messaging and Video Calling

Step 1: Download and Install WeChat

Go to the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android) and search for "WeChat." Download and install the app.

Step 2: Set Up Your Account

  • Open WeChat and tap "Sign Up."
  • Enter your phone number and verify it with the SMS code sent to you.
  • Create a WeChat ID and set up your profile.

Step 3: Sending a Message

  • Tap the chat icon at the bottom of the screen.
  • Select a contact or start a new chat.
  • Type your message and tap send.

Step 4: Making a Video Call

  • Open a chat with the person you want to call.
  • Tap the “+” icon and select “Video Call.”
  • Wait for the other person to answer.
  • Tap the red phone button to end the call.
