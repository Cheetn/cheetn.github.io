Learning Social Media Basics

Social media is a great way to stay connected with family, friends, and the world. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok allow you to share moments, chat with loved ones, and stay updated with news.

How to Use Social Media

  • Creating an Account: Sign up with an email or phone number on platforms like Facebook or TikTok.
  • Posting Content: Share photos, videos, or text updates with your followers.
  • Following Others: Connect with friends and follow people who share interesting content.
  • Interacting: Like, comment, and share posts to engage with others.
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust who can see your content and keep your account secure.

Using TikTok

  • Download the TikTok app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Sign up with an email or phone number.
  • Browse videos by swiping up on the home screen.
  • Like and comment on videos you enjoy.
  • Create your own videos using the record button and apply filters or music.
  • Follow friends or favorite creators to see their content.

Start exploring social media today and stay connected with the digital world!
