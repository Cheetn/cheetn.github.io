Mobile Device Literacy for Seniors

What is a Mobile Device?

Mobile devices include smartphones and tablets. These are portable gadgets that let you call, text, take pictures, access the internet, and use apps.

Common Mobile Devices

  • Smartphones (like iPhone or Samsung Galaxy)
  • Tablets (like iPad or Android tablets)

Basic Mobile Skills

  • Turning the device on/off: Hold the power button for a few seconds.
  • Using the touch screen: Tap icons to open apps. Swipe to scroll or move.
  • Installing apps: Use the App Store (Apple) or Google Play (Android).
  • Making calls and sending texts: Use the Phone and Messages apps.
  • Connecting to Wi-Fi: Go to Settings > Wi-Fi, and select your network.

Understanding Apps

Apps (short for applications) are programs that let you do things like check the weather, watch videos, play games, or manage your health.

Tip: Look for apps with good reviews and lots of downloads. These are usually safe and reliable.

Tips for Using Mobile Devices Safely

  • Set a passcode or fingerprint lock.
  • Be cautious when downloading apps — only from official stores.
  • Update your device regularly.
  • Back up your data to the cloud or computer.
  • Don't share your phone number with strangers.

Fun Things You Can Do

  • Video call your family using WhatsApp or Zoom
  • Watch videos on YouTube
  • Play brain games to stay sharp
  • Read news or books
  • Take and share photos

Quiz: Know Your Device

Question: What should you do if your screen freezes?

  • A. Tap it quickly multiple times
  • B. Try restarting the device
  • C. Drop the phone

Answer: B — Restarting the device often fixes minor problems.

Quiz: App Safety

Question: You see a new app that promises free prizes. What should you do?

  • A. Install it immediately
  • B. Check reviews and source before downloading
  • C. Give your credit card info to register

Answer: B — Always check app safety and never give personal info to unknown sources.

With a little practice, you'll find mobile devices to be powerful tools for staying connected and entertained!
