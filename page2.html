How to Connect to the Internet

To use the internet, follow these simple steps:

  • Go to your device settings and find "Wi-Fi".
  • Select a network and enter the password if required.
  • If using mobile data, enable it in the settings under "Cellular" or "Mobile Data".
  • Once connected, open a web browser to check if the internet is working.

How to Send a Message on WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a free messaging app used worldwide. Here's how you can send a message:

  • Download and install WhatsApp from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Open WhatsApp and verify your phone number.
  • Tap on the chat icon and select a contact.
  • Type your message in the text box.
  • Press the send button (paper plane icon) to deliver the message.

Enjoy chatting with your friends and family on WhatsApp!
